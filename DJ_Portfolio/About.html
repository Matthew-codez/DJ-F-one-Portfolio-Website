About DJ F-One

Shawn Ferreira aka DJ F-One started his journey with music 15 years ago. After joining a band as their resident drummer, Shawn realized that his deep passion for music had many assets. After many years of band touring around Cape Town, he delved into the art of producing and mixing.   He then joined the Kue DJ Academy, to gain valuable knowledge that will later set the foundation for his DJ career.  His vast experience and versatility can be felt in his style of music and skill set. Entertaining crowds for over 15 years and sharing stages with artists like Prime Circle, Pascal & Pearce, Jesse Clegg, Micasa, Jimmy Nevis and many more. He has graced many popular stages and venues across Cape Town, from the CTICC, various Southern Sun Hotels and estates such as Lourensford Wine Estate, Zevenwacht Wine Estate, Cavali, Delaire, Val De Vie and Bakenhof to name a few. He also held various residencies at the iconic Brass Bell Bar, Radisson Red Rooftop Bar and The Southern Sun Cullinan's Stella Bar.  Make him part of your next event and be guaranteed to be entertained.
